Content Delivery Network (CDN)

The Technopark Content Delivery Network (CDN) caches static content at strategically placed locations to provide maximum throughput for delivering content to users. The CDN offers innovators a global solution for delivering high-innovative content by caching the content at physical nodes across the world.
